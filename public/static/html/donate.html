The Honor System

Hi, I'm Jimmy, the author of Online Font Converter! That's me and my brother Stefan in the picture.

Online font converter is "honor-ware", which means that we trust each other to be nice:

  • You pay what you can afford to convert a font. I let you choose the price.
  • I'll send you a thank you note for paying -- and more, if you pay more.

I quit my job to work on Online Font Converter, so we're trusting you a lot, but we think it's the decent thing to do.
